Okra masala curry

bhindi masala recipe | bhindi ki gravy recipe | okra masala curry recipe

Description

Okra or bhindi is a versatile vegetable and is used in different types of indian recipes. even though it is very well appreciated in indian cuisine, it can be tricky to prepare as it releases the sticky latex while cooking. keeping all those in considerations, i am posting a rich and creamy gravy recipe known as bhindi masala recipe or okra masala curry.
       People complain about the sticky latex okra releases while cooking this gravy. I do understand it can be tricky to handle okra, hence i thought of re-sharing this post again with additional tips and suggestions. The first tip is to keep the okra dry. We generally wash the vegetables before using them. Firstly, we have to wash okra too but have to thoroughly wipe and dry it before slit and stuffing. The other tip is to fry it before mixing it to the gravy base. This step helps to remove all the moisture within the okra. also, it also cuts the direct contact of fresh okra with the moisture from the gravy base. I would strongly recommend not skipping this step for a perfect masala gravy base.

Ingredients

    for frying bhindi:
  • 10 bhindi, chopped
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp oil
    • for curry:
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 8 clove garlic
  • 2 tsp ginger paste
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • 1.5 cup tomato puree
  • ¼ cup curd
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ onion, petals
  • 1 tomato, quarter
  • ¼ tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Steps

  1. firstly, take 10 bhindi and chop them into pieces. make sure to slit in the centre so that masala can be absorbed.
  2. add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala.
  3. mix well coating all the spices to bhindi.
  4. in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil, add 1 tsp kasuri methi, 1 tsp cumin and saute until the spices turn aromatic.
  5. now add 1 onion, 8 clove garlic and 1 tsp ginger paste.
  6. saute well making sure the onions turn golden brown.
  7. further add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder and ½ tsp cumin powder.
  8. saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  9. add 1½ cup tomato puree and saute well.
  10. saute until the oil separates from the puree.
  11. now add ¼ cup curd and cook until the oil separates.
  12. add 1 cup water and 1 tsp salt. mix well adjusting the consistency of the curry.
  13. in another pan heat 2 tsp oil and fry the bhindi.
  14. fry until the bhindi changes colour and is almost cooked.
  15. transfer the fried bhindi to the curry, add ½ onion and 1 tomato.
  16. mix well, cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until the flavours are absorbed well.
  17. add ¼ tsp garam masala and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well.
  18. finally, enjoy bhindi masala recipe with roti.
